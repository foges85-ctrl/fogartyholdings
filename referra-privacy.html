Referra
Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 22, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Referra app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices, created by Sean Fogarty (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a freemium service with optional in-app purchases. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Short version: Referra stores your contact and referral data in your own personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit. The Service Provider has no ability to access, view, or recover your data. No analytics, no tracking, no third-party services.

Information Collection and Storage

Referra is designed for rehab and performance professionals (physical therapists, athletic trainers, chiropractors, personal trainers, and coaches) to manage referral relationships. The Application does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information with the Service Provider or any third party. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking SDKs are included
  • No usage data is sent to the Service Provider
  • No account creation or login with the Service Provider is required
  • No advertising is served within the Application
  • No artificial intelligence (AI) services process your data

When you use the Application, you may enter the following information about your professional contacts and relationships, all of which is stored exclusively in your own personal iCloud account:

  • Contact details for referral sources, past clients, and colleagues (names, roles, organizations, phone numbers, email addresses)
  • Notes about each contact (non-clinical only — see below)
  • Logs of interactions you have with each contact (calls, emails, meetings)
  • Reminders and follow-up schedules
  • Records of referrals you receive, including optional context tags
  • Your selected profession (chosen during onboarding)

Where Your Data Lives

The Application uses Apple's CloudKit framework to store your data in the private database of your personal iCloud account. This means:

  • Your data is stored in your own iCloud, not on servers operated by the Service Provider
  • The Service Provider has no access to your data and cannot view, retrieve, modify, or delete it
  • Your data syncs automatically across your own Apple devices that are signed in to the same iCloud account
  • Apple's CloudKit terms and privacy practices govern the storage and transmission of your data; see Apple's Privacy Policy for details

If you are not signed in to iCloud, the Application will store your data locally on your device only.

Not a Medical Record System

Referra is explicitly designed as a relationship management tool, not a medical record system. The Application is not HIPAA-covered and is not intended to store Protected Health Information (PHI) about patients or clients.

You are responsible for ensuring that information you enter into the Application does not include patient-identifying clinical data, treatment notes, diagnoses, or other PHI. Onboarding screens and form fields within the Application reinforce this guidance. Clinical information should remain in your Electronic Medical Record (EMR) or practice management system.

In-App Purchases

The Application offers optional paid features through Apple's In-App Purchase system, including:

  • Pro Monthly subscription
  • Pro Annual subscription
  • Pro Lifetime (one-time purchase)

All payment processing is handled directly by Apple through your Apple ID. The Service Provider does not receive, see, or store your payment information, credit card details, or billing address. The Service Provider receives only a confirmation from Apple that a purchase has been made, which is used solely to grant access to Pro features within the Application.

For information about how Apple processes your payment data, see Apple's Privacy Policy.

Notifications

The Application uses Apple's local notifications framework to remind you about scheduled follow-ups. These notifications are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device. No notification content is transmitted to the Service Provider or to Apple's push notification servers (silent CloudKit sync notifications are managed by Apple and contain no user-identifiable content).

Third Party Access

The Application does not share any data with third parties. The only external services involved are:

  • Apple iCloud / CloudKit — for storing and syncing your data within your own iCloud account
  • Apple In-App Purchase — for processing optional Pro purchases

Both are governed by Apple's privacy practices, not the Service Provider's. The Service Provider may disclose information only as required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process. However, as no user data is collected or stored by the Service Provider, there is no data to disclose.

Children

The Application is intended for use by professional adults managing their business relationships and is not directed at children under the age of 13. The Service Provider does not knowingly solicit data from or market to children.

If you have questions about your child's use of the Application, please contact the Service Provider at contact@fogartyholdings.com.

Data Retention and Deletion

All data entered into the Application is stored in your own iCloud account and is under your full control. To delete your data, you can:

  • Delete individual contacts, interactions, reminders, or referral events from within the Application
  • Delete the Application from your device — note that this removes the app but your iCloud-stored data may persist until manually cleared from your iCloud account
  • Manage iCloud storage and remove app data via Settings → [your name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage on your Apple device

The Service Provider does not retain any user data and therefore cannot perform deletion requests on your behalf.

Security

Because data is stored in your personal iCloud, security is governed by Apple's iCloud infrastructure, which includes encryption in transit and at rest. Authentication to your iCloud account is controlled by your Apple ID credentials. The Service Provider has no access to your iCloud data and does not maintain any servers that store your information.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. The Service Provider will notify you of any changes by updating this page with the new Privacy Policy. You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes, as continued use is deemed approval of all changes.

Your Consent

By using the Application, you are consenting to the processing of your information as set forth in this Privacy Policy now and as amended by us.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Application, or have questions about our practices, please contact the Service Provider via email at contact@fogartyholdings.com.
