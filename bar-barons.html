Features

Verified visits.

🧾
Receipt-verified claims

On-device OCR confirms you were actually there. Apple's Vision framework reads the receipt locally — no faking, no manual check-ins.

👑
Throne competition

Become Baron of a bar (most visits citywide) or Lord of a neighborhood (most visits in your patch). Defend your title, or take someone else's.

🪜
Tier progression

Newcomer → Patron → Regular. Every verified visit pushes you up a bar's ladder. Show off where you actually drink.

👥
Friend feed

Mutual-confirm friends to see each other's activity in a shared feed. Compare turfs, plan a night, settle who really runs the East Village.

🗽
1,800+ bars covered

All five NYC boroughs plus Westchester County — dive bars, cocktail rooms, beer gardens, neighborhood mainstays. From the East Village to St. George to Yonkers.

🔒
Privacy-first

Your data lives in your iCloud, not on our servers. No analytics, no ads, no trackers. Sign in with Apple keeps your real name and email yours.
