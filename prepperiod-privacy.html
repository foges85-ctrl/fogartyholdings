PrepPeriod
Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 10, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the PrepPeriod app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices, created by Fogarty Holdings LLC (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a free service with an optional one-time in-app purchase. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

PrepPeriod is a classroom documentation tool for teachers. It lets you capture classroom events — behavior incidents, hall passes, positive notes, parent-contact records, and attendance — from your iPhone and Apple Watch, and complete the details later. All of the information you enter stays on your own devices and within your own private iCloud account.

Short version: PrepPeriod stores everything you enter — including any student and class information — on your own devices and in your own personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit. The Service Provider has no ability to access, view, or recover your data. There are no accounts, no developer servers, no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party data sharing. Your data is never sold or shared. Ever.

Information Collection and Storage

The Application is designed with privacy as its core principle. It does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information with the Service Provider or any third party. Specifically:

  • No data is sent to servers operated by the Service Provider — there are none
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs are included
  • The Application does not track you across other apps or websites
  • No account creation or login with the Service Provider is required
  • No advertising is served within the Application
  • No Artificial Intelligence (AI) service processes your data

When you use the Application, you may enter the following information about your classes and students, all of which is stored exclusively on your own devices and in your own personal iCloud account:

  • Student identifiers you choose to use (such as names or initials)
  • Behavior incidents and positive notes
  • Hall pass logs (for example bathroom, water, or nurse) and their timestamps
  • Attendance records, including rotating and A/B day schedules
  • Parent-contact records, including the date, method, and outcome of contact
  • Class, roster, and schedule details you set up

Every entry is timestamped on your device at the moment you capture it. This information is entered by you and remains under your control at all times.

Where Your Data Lives

The Application stores your data locally on your device and uses Apple's CloudKit framework to store and sync it in the private database of your personal iCloud account. This means:

  • Your data is stored on your own iPhone and Apple Watch and in your own iCloud, not on servers operated by the Service Provider
  • The Service Provider has no access to your data and cannot view, retrieve, modify, or delete it
  • Your data syncs automatically across your own Apple devices that are signed in to the same iCloud account
  • Apple's CloudKit terms and privacy practices govern the storage and transmission of your data; see Apple's Privacy Policy for details

If you are not signed in to iCloud, the Application stores your data locally on your device only.

Student Information and Your Responsibilities

PrepPeriod is designed so that any information you record about students — who may be minors — never leaves your control. It is stored on your own devices and in your own private iCloud account, is never transmitted to the Service Provider, and is not accessible to the Service Provider or any third party.

Because you enter and maintain this information, you are the controller of the student data within the Application. You are responsible for handling student information in accordance with your school's or district's policies and with all applicable laws and regulations, which may include the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA) and other student-privacy or data-protection laws that apply to you.

The Service Provider does not act as a "school official," does not receive an educational record, and has no access to any student information you enter. You should record only the information you are permitted to record, use student identifiers consistent with your school's guidance (for example, initials where appropriate), and follow your institution's requirements for retaining and disclosing student records.

Apple Watch

PrepPeriod is built to let you capture events from your Apple Watch. Data captured on your Apple Watch is stored on the watch and syncs with your iPhone and your private iCloud account using Apple's frameworks. As with all Application data, it is never transmitted to the Service Provider.

Notifications

The Application uses Apple's local notifications framework to remind you to complete your captured entries — for example, during your prep period. These notifications are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device. No notification content is transmitted to the Service Provider (silent CloudKit sync notifications are managed by Apple and contain no user-identifiable content).

In-App Purchases

The Application is free to download and use. It offers an optional one-time in-app purchase that unlocks professional PDF report export. This purchase is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store using StoreKit.

  • The Service Provider does not receive, process, or store any payment information, credit card details, or billing address
  • All payment data is handled exclusively by Apple under Apple's Privacy Policy
  • The Service Provider receives only a confirmation from Apple that a purchase has been made, which is used solely to unlock the feature; your purchase and entitlement state is managed through Apple's StoreKit and stored on your device
  • Restoring a previous purchase is performed via Apple's standard restore flow and likewise involves no Service Provider servers

Any PDF reports you generate are created on your device. What you do with an exported report — such as printing, saving, or sharing it — is under your control and subject to your school's policies and applicable laws.

Third Party Access

The Application does not share any data with third parties, and no data is ever sold or shared. The only external services involved are:

  • Apple iCloud / CloudKit — for storing and syncing your data within your own iCloud account
  • Apple In-App Purchase — for processing the optional one-time PDF report unlock

Both are governed by Apple's privacy practices, not the Service Provider's. The Service Provider may disclose information only as required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process. However, as no user data is collected or stored by the Service Provider, there is no user data to disclose.

Children

The Application is intended for use by teachers and other education professionals, and is not directed at children. The Service Provider does not knowingly solicit data from or market to children, and does not collect any personally identifiable information from any user.

PrepPeriod may be used by a teacher to record information about students, who may be minors. As described above, this information is entered by the teacher, never leaves the teacher's own devices and private iCloud account, and is never transmitted to or accessible by the Service Provider. If you have questions about a child's information in connection with the Application, please contact the Service Provider at contact@fogartyholdings.com.

Data Retention and Deletion

All data entered into the Application is stored on your own devices and in your own iCloud account and is under your full control. To delete your data, you can:

  • Delete individual entries, students, or classes from within the Application
  • Delete the Application from your device — note that this removes the app but your iCloud-stored data may persist until manually cleared from your iCloud account
  • Manage iCloud storage and remove app data via Settings → [your name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage on your Apple device

The Service Provider does not retain any user data and therefore cannot perform deletion requests on your behalf.

Security

The Service Provider has designed the Application from the ground up with security as a core principle. Your data remains on your device and within your own iCloud account, where security is governed by Apple's iOS and iCloud infrastructure, including encryption in transit and at rest. Authentication to your iCloud account is controlled by your Apple ID credentials. The Application makes no outbound network connections beyond Apple's iCloud sync and the standard StoreKit transactions handled by Apple, and the Service Provider maintains no servers that store your information.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. The Service Provider will notify you of any changes by updating this page with the new Privacy Policy. You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes, as continued use is deemed approval of all changes.

Your Consent

By using the Application, you are consenting to the processing of your information as set forth in this Privacy Policy now and as amended by us.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Application, or have questions about our practices, please contact the Service Provider via email at contact@fogartyholdings.com.
