FAQ

Common questions.

Is it really 100% offline?

Yes. The app requires no account and no internet connection. It makes zero network calls, so it has no technical ability to transmit your information anywhere. Everything stays on your device.

What can I store in it?

Emergency contacts sorted by priority, your final wishes and medical directives, current medications and allergies, the locations of important documents like your will and accounts, and a personal letter to your executor.

How does the PDF export work?

You can generate a professionally formatted PDF at any time — with your name, date, document ID, executor letter, and a confidentiality footer. Print it and store it with your important papers, or share it securely with your executor.

What is an Apple Legacy Contact?

It's an Apple feature that lets someone request access to your iPhone and account after you pass away, without your passcode or Face ID. When you set a primary emergency contact, the app guides you through designating them.

Is my data encrypted?

Yes. All data is encrypted at rest using iOS Complete File Protection, your vault locks behind Face ID or Touch ID, and it's excluded from iCloud backup by design.

Do I need to create an account?

No. There are no accounts and no sign-up. You download the app, set up your vault, and everything lives privately on your device from the start.
