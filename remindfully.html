Why It's Different

The problem was never
capturing reminders.

You already capture reminders without thinking about it. Siri takes them on the way out the door. Your Apple Watch grabs one mid-walk. The Reminders app is a tap away. That part works.

The pain comes next — the wall of notifications that follows. A steady drip all day, every item shouting with the same urgency, until the genuinely important ones are buried in the same pile as "buy paper towels."

Remindfully sits on top of Apple Reminders and changes only one thing: how your reminders reach you. It reads your existing reminders and replaces the all-day pile-up with a single morning digest — while still letting the things that actually matter interrupt at the right moment.

Your reminders never move. Siri, Apple Watch, and the Reminders app keep working exactly as before. There's nothing to migrate, nothing to re-enter, and nothing to give up.

Free forever for the digest and interruptions. Remindfully Pro is a one-time $4.99 — no subscription.

FAQ

Common questions.

Does it replace Apple Reminders?

No. Remindfully sits on top of Apple Reminders and only changes how they reach you. Your reminders stay in Apple Reminders, and Siri, Apple Watch, and the Reminders app keep working exactly as before.

Do I have to move my reminders over?

No. There's nothing to migrate. Remindfully reads the reminders you already have. Keep capturing them however you do today — by Siri, Watch, or the Reminders app — and Remindfully simply changes how they're delivered.

How does the daily digest work?

Once each morning, Remindfully sends a single summary notification of everything due today. Instead of alerts trickling in all day, you get one calm read and a clear picture of what's ahead.

What still breaks through the quiet?

Two things: reminders with a specific time interrupt at that time, and anything you mark High Priority always comes through. Everything else waits politely for the next digest.

What happens if I miss something?

A missed daily reminder quietly rolls forward to today so it isn't lost. A missed weekly or monthly reminder stays clearly flagged rather than slipping away — so a missed bill doesn't silently vanish for weeks.

Is my data private?

Yes. Everything happens on your device and in your own iCloud account. Fogarty Holdings never sees or stores your reminders. No tracking, no ads, no accounts, no servers.

What's free, and what's Pro?

Free forever: the digest, time-based and high-priority interruptions, completion sync, and the reminder mirror. Pro adds the lock-screen card and per-list rules — a one-time $4.99, no subscription.

Is there a subscription?

No. Remindfully Pro is a single one-time purchase of $4.99, handled entirely by Apple. Pay once and it's yours — and your unlock follows your Apple ID across devices.
